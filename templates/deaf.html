Deaf, Dumb, and Blind Awareness

Deaf Awareness

Deafness is a condition where a person has little to no hearing ability. It can be caused by various factors such as genetics, illness, or aging. People who are deaf communicate through sign language, lip reading, and written forms of communication.

Speech Impairment Awareness

Speech impairment involves difficulties in speaking due to physical, neurological, or developmental conditions. Many people with speech impairments communicate through sign language or writing.

Blindness Awareness

Blindness refers to the condition of having no vision or very limited vision. Many blind individuals rely on braille, audio cues, and special tools to navigate the world.
