Why This Website?

This website is designed to help people who may have visual impairments or any disability that makes it hard to interpret images. It uses AI to describe images in detail and convert them into audio, making the content more accessible to everyone.

What It Does

This website allows you to upload an image, and our AI-powered system processes the image to generate a text description. This description is then converted into an audio file, making it easier for people to understand and enjoy images.

How It Works

Once you upload an image, our system uses image recognition technology to detect objects and scenes within the image. It then generates a detailed description, which is converted into audio. You can listen to the audio through a built-in audio player on the site.
